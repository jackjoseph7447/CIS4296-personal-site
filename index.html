Projects

I've worked on projects that I am proud of.
Final Grade Calculator for my high school

The purpose of this app was to allow students to see the grade they would need their final for their respective class. By doing this, they can ease some of the nervousness that surrounds finals season for many schools around around the world. By taking the grades that the student has earned throughout the the semester, the app will put those numbers into a formula and display the grade needed to earn on a final to get the grade they desire.

Password Storing app

Although this app was not finished due to lack of time, we had this modeled out and knew what needed to be done. We had a login page where you could even create an account that would then be stored in a google database. After that stage, it would have the list of passwords that you created, and you could create them as well.
